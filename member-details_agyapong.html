Alfred Anane Agyapong

Chief Executive

Alfred Anane Agyapong was born in Techiman in the Bono East Region of Ghana to Mr. Thomas Tuffour and Madam Elizabeth Nsowaa. He had his basic education from R/C primary and Junior High schools and moved on to Agona SDA high school. He later had his Tertiary education at Community College of Aurora Colorado, USA.

He is marrried to Mrs Joy Agyapong with children.

He worked in Ghana as a national fire service officer and also at the Volta River Authority.

In the United States of America, he also did Film Studies course at the Denver Film School. He held several positions in many American Film and TV companies

He is currently the Executive Chairman and financier for PB Radio and films, Executive Director ALFPI Company limited USA, Chief Executive for Famous Multimedia Ghana and USA.

Mr Agyapong has worked with great Personalities and top actors in Africa including Africa Movie Academy winners such as Majeed Michelle, Jim Ike, Emelia Brobbey, Kojo Nkansah Lil Win, Efo Lucky Caleb, Haruna Zoree Dialed, Mclord Ice, Prince David Osei, Afrikaba Ronnie and many more.

His profession has taken him accross the world especially America, Europe, Asia and African countries.

He has won many awards both Ghana and abroad including "All Best Round Officer at Fire Training Academy 2022, Ghana and Recruit Award/Best in Academics.

His films 'GHETTO HERO', 'BAD GAYL', 'GALAMSEY', 'THE BIG SIX' and 'ZONGO BOYS' also won many awards at Ghana Movie Awards and at many film festivals abroad.

He has attained many great things for himself and still making great input to restructure the Ghana film industry.

He has been helping many needy and youth in education, job creation and career orientation. He is known to be regular donor to orphanages and old age homes in the rural areas.

His biggest aspiration is to build a strong film company that will promote Africa stories to the world and by so means create more sustainable employment for the youth.

He is also looking into venturing into Estate building and Recreational Centres in the nearest future.

Many people aspire him from afar in life but the people who pushes him to do more in life are his wife, children, best friend Efo Lucky Caleb, Mr. Pius Sarpong and the parents.

Alfred Agyapong is one of the high rated Ghanaian Entertainment business personalities doing so well and expected to capture the world with his works soon.
